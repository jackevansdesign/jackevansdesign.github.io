Summer 2017

In 1917 Sigmund Freud discovered the unconscious forces that govern us all and suddenly we were no longer ourselves:

The ego is not master in its own house.

One century later, our minds having been reengineered for consent, we upload digital husks of who we once were, precious data mined by corporations pretending to be people.

The future will be posthuman.

Early storyboards for the Idiot Box, a three component ad interface (logo, background, tagline) bookended by loading and engagement screens.
