Auditing visual assets for modular display ads

Effective display ads have four components: logo, tagline, call-to-action, and background image.

Auditing visual assets into strictly modular components clarifies and streamlines ad interfaces.

Display ads lack engagement when they are unfamiliar and out of context. Modular, component-based ad interfaces are metrically scalable and self-evident, familiarizing themselves to users with each impression by mere-exposure effect.
