Shop

Cross everyone off your nice list while avoiding mall parking lot madness.

Shop handmade and local at Fenton Street Market’s HOLIDAZE pop up shop at Pyramid Atlantic. Open Dec. 4-23, stop by for gift giving made simple, plus classes and parties! Find all the products you love to buy at FSM, in one fun shop.

Or brave the outdoors and shop festive outdoor markets on Dec. 3, 10, 17 (in front of Whole Foods) and 19 (on Veterans Plaza). At each market you’ll find local makers selling one of a kind gifts. Win the holidays this year with handmade!

Get your retail therapy at Third Space Wellness on Dec. 6 at their Local with Love Holiday Bazaar. Bring the whole family for a stress-free holiday shopping experience and enjoy hot chocolate, kids activities and chair massages plus unique gifts from local women entrepreneurs!

Gift certificates are the way to go! Give the gift of relaxation with a voucher for acupuncture, massages and yoga at Third Space Wellness or the gift of creativity with a voucher for a class or membership at Catylator Makerspace. Reward your employees with: passes to the AFI Silver so they can catch up on indie flicks, gift cards to Denizens so they can forget all about work over some beers.

For your beer loving friends pair that gift card with some Denizens swag. Rep your brewery with a hoodie or tee that’ll show your Silver Spring pride far and wide.

Stop by the Silver Spring Books on Bonifant Street and score coffee table tomes for cheap or load up on old classics for your literary friends. Or swing around the corner to Alliance for comics and action figures.

Got a music lover on your list? Check out Silver Spring’s very own independent record label Cuneiform Records and peruse their online shop Wayside Music for adventurous albums by avant-garde artists from all over the world.
