Indulge

Treat yourself. You only live once and resolutions are for January.

Take those out of town guests on the brewery tour at Denizens and then stay to try their new dinner menu. You know, there is no beer at the Washington Monument... It’s a win-win: they’re impressed, everyone’s buzzed and family dynamics take a back seat to great beer and food.

After a long day of making at Catylator Makerspace or shopping at HOLIDAZE, stop by Kaldi’s Social House for one of their amazing desserts made in house. Chocolate extravaganza anyone?

Shop for yourself at HOLIDAZE! Or if you want to make sure you get just what you want this holiday season, fill out a wish list and send your loved ones back to buy the gifts you’ve had your eye on.

Take care of your body with a massage at Third Space Wellness or a facial and manicure at Spa Mesu. A quick break from the holiday madness will feel like bliss and then you’ll be ready to go back to playing host.

Celebrate the holidays over a bottle of wine and truffle fries at Adega Wine Cellars. Kick back and enjoy the view of all those brave skaters down on the ice rink.

Leave the office early and hit up one of the many great happy hours in Silver Spring. La Malinche has killer sangria and tapas. If you need a drink to make you forget your cares, the Zombie at Lotus Cafe is for you. El Golfo in Long Branch offers the cheapest beer and nachos around (plus bilingual karaoke on Mondays!). And of course Denizens is the spot for local beer.
